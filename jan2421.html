By Tom McCoy

Directions: Answer the clues below the grid. Each letter that you enter will fill in one square in the grid; when completed, the grid will spell a quotation reading from left to right, top to bottom. The first letters of the answers will spell out the source of the quotation.

You can enter letters into any of the three components of the puzzle and those letters will automatically be added to the other two parts; e.g., if you fill in a letter in the source of the quotation, it will be added to the corresponding spots in the grid and in the clue answers.

If you need a hint, click the eye icon in the top right of the grid to reveal a letter, a word, or the entire puzzle.
